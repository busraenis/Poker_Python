Question:

Poker

Please read important information first.

Description

There are 52 cards in a deck, 13 from each suit (Diamonds, Clubs, Hearts, Spades).
The cards are valued in the order: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 (T), Jack, Queen, King, Ace.
Cards are represented as their suit and their value combination. Ex : AH = Ace of Hearts, 8C = 8 of Clubs ...
In the card game poker, a hand consists of five cards and are ranked, from highest to lowest, in the following way:

 10 - Royal Flush: Ten, Jack, Queen, King, Ace, in same suit. It is special case of Straight Flush (Example. AH JH KH QH TH)
 9 - Straight Flush: All cards are consecutive values of same suit. (Example. 8C 4C 6C 5C 7C)
 8 - Four of a Kind: Four cards of the same value. (Example. 4S 4D 5D 4C 4H)
 7 - Full House: Three of a kind and a pair. (Example. 7H AH AC 7S AD)
 6 - Flush: All cards of the same suit. (Example. KD 6D 8D JD TD)
 5 - Straight: All cards are consecutive values. (Example. JC TS 7S 8S 9D)
 4 - Three of a Kind: Three cards of the same value. (Example. 9S TC TS 6H TH)
 3 - Two Pairs: Two different pairs. (Example. 5C AD 5D AC 9C)
 2 - Pair: Two cards of the same value. (Example. 9H 4D JC KS JS)
 1 - High Card: None of the above. (Example. 7C 5H KC QH JD)

For given hands of poker, you are expected to print the highest ranked category they fall into.
You should read the hands from a file. This file's handler is stored in the file variable.
First value in the file is the number of hands, which is stored in number_of_hands variable.
Remaining rows contains the cards for each hand, split by a space.
To output the ranked category, use the provided "print_result" function. This function takes the rank of the category (Ex : 6 for Flush) and prints it's name.

Warning: You are not allowed to use any imports.
